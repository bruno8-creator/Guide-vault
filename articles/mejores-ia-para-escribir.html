If you want to write faster without sounding generic, the best AI tool depends on the type of writing: ideas, emails, articles, social posts, scripts, or final editing.

Which one should you choose?

For most users, start with ChatGPT or Claude. ChatGPT is a flexible all-purpose assistant for almost any writing task. Claude is especially useful when you need longer, coherent text with a natural tone.

Recommended prompt

Do not ask only “write an article”. Give audience, goal, tone, format, length, and style examples. The quality of the output depends heavily on the instructions.
