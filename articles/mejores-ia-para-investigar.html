Best AI tools for research and finding sources

For research, the most important thing is not a pretty answer. You need sources, verification, and a clear difference between facts and interpretation.

PerplexityAnswers with sourcesSearches the web and synthesizes answers with links.

Which one should you choose?

Do not copy an AI answer without opening the sources. Use AI to find, summarize, and organize; use your own judgment to verify.

Recommended prompt

Prompt: “Research this topic using reliable sources. Separate confirmed facts, likely interpretations, and open questions.”
