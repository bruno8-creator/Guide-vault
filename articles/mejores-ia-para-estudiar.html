Best AI tools for studying and learning faster

AI can help you study better if you use it as a tutor, not as a replacement for thinking. It can explain concepts, create questions, summarize notes, and reveal gaps in your understanding.

ToolBest forWhy it stands out
ChatGPTStep-by-step tutoringAsk for explanations, examples, exercises, and exam-style questions.
GeminiGeneral study supportUseful if you already work with Google tools and documents.
PerplexityResearch with sourcesGood for updated answers and links you can verify.
NotebookLMYour own documentsUseful for studying PDFs, class notes, and uploaded material.
QuizletMemorization and flashcardsHelpful for vocabulary, definitions, and quick review.

Which one should you choose?

Try solving the problem first. Then use AI to compare your reasoning. Ask it to quiz you, not only to give you answers.

Recommended prompt

Prompt: “Explain this topic as if I am a beginner. Then ask me 10 questions with increasing difficulty and correct my answers.”
